# Unit3: 比較

## 比較級

### 強調

比較級を橋梁する場合は、以下の語句を比較級の前に用いる。

- a little
- a bit
- slightly
- a lot
- much
- far
- even

The test was slightly more difficult than I thought.  
その試験は思っていたよりちょっとだけ難しかった。

## 最上級

複数の中で最上を示す場合、当然ながら複数形を用いる。

New york is one of the most interesting **cities** in the world.

### 最上級と比較級の相互表現

以下の表現はすべて同じ意味になる。

- 最上級「もっとも〜だ」  
  Mt. Fuji is the most popular mountain in Japan.
- No other 単数名詞 動詞 as 原級 as 〜「〜ほど原級な単数名詞は無い」  
  No other mountain in Japan is as popular as Mt. Fuji.
- No other 単数名詞 動詞 比較級 then 〜「〜よりも比較級な単数名詞はない」  
  No other mountain in Japan is more popular than Mt. Fuji.
- 比較級 than any other 単数名詞「どの単数名詞よりも比較級だ」  
  Mt. Fuji is more popular than any other mountain in Japan.

## as A as 〜の多彩な表現

### 倍数表現: 倍数 as 原級 as

This new computer warks twice as fast as the old one.

### 強調表現

### 同等表現：AとBと同じくらい

He speaks as naturally as a native speaker.
彼はネイティブと同じくらい自然に話す。

Mr. Simpson is one of the most trusted people in our department.

２文目はパターンが多い

- 最上級を使う  
  And he has the most experience and knowledge in the industry than anyone here.
- 比較級＋than any other 単数名詞を使う  
  And he has more experience and knowledge in the industry than anyone here.
- No other + 比較級を使う  
  And no one has more experience and knowledge in the industry than him here.
- No other + 原級を使う  
  And no one has has as much as experience and knowledge in the industry than him here.
